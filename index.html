Our story

We are a small coffee shop founded in 2010 by two friends who shared a love for the magical drink.

Ten years and many cups later we arrive here. With our love for both coffee and people alike, we ensure to put our magic in every single bean that is grounded.

We also love this planet so every bean is Fairtrade and we offer vegan alternatives on all our range of coffees and some of our cakes too. Come down and enjoy the wonderful elixir.
